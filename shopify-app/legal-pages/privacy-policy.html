Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

Contact: privacy@mailcraft-editor.pages.dev

1. Data We Collect

When you install MailCraft, we collect and process the following data on your behalf:

  • Product images — retrieved via the Shopify Admin API to generate social-media creatives.
  • Product titles and descriptions — used as text overlays in generated images.
  • Access tokens — OAuth access tokens issued by Shopify to authenticate API calls on your behalf.
  • Shop domain, plan name, and locale settings.

2. Data Retention

  • Access tokens: deleted on uninstall via the shop/redact and app/uninstalled webhooks.
  • Generated images: retained for 90 days after creation, then automatically deleted from R2 storage.
  • Logs and trace events: retained for 90 days via Cloudflare Logpush R2 lifecycle rules.
  • D1 database records (merchant rows, product metadata): deleted within 48 hours of receiving a shop/redact webhook.

3. Third-Party Processors

We share data with the following sub-processors to deliver the service:

  • Cloudflare — Workers compute, D1 database, R2 storage, KV, and Logpush.
  • Remove.bg — background removal for product images (product image URLs are sent; no PII is shared).
  • Resend — transactional email delivery for billing receipts and alerts.
  • Sentry — error monitoring and performance tracing (no customer PII is included in error payloads).

4. GDPR Mandatory Webhooks

As required by Shopify's Partner Agreement, we implement all three GDPR webhooks:

  • customers/data_request — returns all data held for a customer within 30 days.
  • customers/redact — deletes all personal data associated with a customer.
  • shop/redact — deletes all shop data 48 hours after an uninstall is confirmed.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@mailcraft-editor.pages.dev.
